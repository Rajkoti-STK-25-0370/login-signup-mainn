Drag & drop website builder FAQ

A drag & drop website builder is a user-friendly tool that lets you design and customize your website by dragging and dropping elements onto your page. It simplifies the website creation, so you don't need coding skills.
The best drag-and-drop website builder depends on your needs. Stackly is widely considered the top choice due to its ease of use, design flexibility, and wide range of features. For users who prioritize design and aesthetics, Squarespace is an excellent option. Overall, the best builder is one that combines simplicity, customization, and the tools you need to grow your website.
Stackly offers flexible pricing designed to suit different needs and budgets. It typically includes a free plan to get started, along with affordable paid plans that scale as your requirements grow, with more advanced plans available at $150/month, $180/month, and higher tiers for larger businesses or enterprise use.
With a drag-and-drop website builder like Stackly, you can create a wide variety of websites without any coding. This includes business websites, portfolios, blogs, landing pages, eCommerce stores, service-based websites, and even event or educational sites. The flexibility of the builder allows you to customize layouts, add features, and design pages to match your specific goals, making it suitable for individuals, startups, and growing businesses alike.
A drag-and-drop website builder offers several key advantages, including ease of use, speed, and flexibility. You can quickly design pages, move elements, and see changes in real time. It also provides ready-made templates, built-in features like SEO and mobile responsiveness, and the ability to update your site anytime, making it an ideal solution for individuals, startups, and businesses looking to build a professional online presence efficiently.
Building a website with a drag-and-drop builder is quick and efficient. A basic website can be created in just a few hours, while a more detailed and fully customized site may take a few days depending on the content, design, and features required. Overall, it significantly reduces development time compared to traditional coding, allowing you to launch your website much faster.
